# Overview

## Concept

Orbitfall is a scenario-overhaul-style Oxygen Not Included: Spaced Out mod where the player starts inside a damaged ship or ship-like habitat on finite reserves. Early progress comes from consuming finite reserves while restoring critical routes and building constrained extensions under pressure rather than inheriting a mostly working colony.

## Core Fantasy

- A mission has gone wrong.
- The crew is buying time, not building comfort.
- Every short-term fix should feel like survival triage.
- Survival comes from buying time and bringing critical functionality online, not settling into comfort.
- Progress means earning a risky transition to planetary operations.
- The world remains hostile even after industrial growth.

## Identity Targets

- Distinct phase identity from opening to late game.
- Strong pressure without obscuring core ONI system readability.
- Familiar tools in unfamiliar constraints.
- Short replayable opening burst rather than a long mandatory scripted intro.
- Planetary hostility remains relevant through late game.
- Early gameplay focuses on restore-then-extend patterns rather than immediate sustainability.

## Scope Position

- Single mod project focused on one coherent scenario arc.
- Spaced Out DLC is required.
- Initial implementation path centers on one hazard, with heat as the first target.
- Future hazard variety is possible later but not assumed for first delivery.

## Scenario Arc

- Damaged ship or ship-like habitat survival and repair.
- Descent readiness and judgment.
- Hostile beachhead establishment.
- Sustained hostile-world industrial operation.

Core challenge is driven by world conditions rather than altering how machines function.
